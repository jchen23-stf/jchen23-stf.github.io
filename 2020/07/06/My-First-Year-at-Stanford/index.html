My First Year at Stanford
Created 2020-07-06| Updated 2020-07-19
Post View:

Intro

My first year at Stanford was like a roller-coaster. I came to Stanford as a confused international student, worked really hard to find my balance, and then left Stanford suddenly in a global pandemic. Needless to say, my freshman experience was one-of-its-kind - I met so many brilliant minds and explored some of the most wonderful opportunities, all of which have last a lasting impact on me.

Class

Classes at Stanford are amazing. Because of the one university policy, I was virtually able to enroll in any class (except wine-tasting and social dance). I decided to take a variety of classes as in different departments, class sizes, and formats. All of the classes were extremely rewarding - I was able to learn in and out of class room.
